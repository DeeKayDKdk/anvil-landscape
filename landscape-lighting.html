Why it's worth doing right

Anyone can stake cheap lights in the ground.

The difference between that and a finished, professional result comes down to four things.

🔩

Solid brass, not aluminum

Aluminum fixtures corrode and need replacing in a few seasons. Our brass fixtures are built to last — and carry a lifetime warranty.

💛

Warm 2700K glow

The rich, golden light of a fine hotel — not the harsh blue-white of a big-box kit. It makes brick, bark, and stone look their best.

🪱

Every wire buried

No cords across the lawn, nothing to trip on or mow over. The system disappears — only the light remains.

⏱️

Fully automatic

A professional timer and dusk-to-dawn sensor switches everything on at sunset and off on schedule. You never touch it.
